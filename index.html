Welcome!

GuardU is a web-based portal and Learning Management System (LMS) that allows Soldiers, units, training institutions, and States to access an online environment for directed learning and self-development.

If you experience an issue or would like to host content, please contact GuardU Customer Support at 844-722-2373.
